Who are we?

Eyeline Bussiness and Development is a Financial software development company specialized in the economic sector of crypto assets.

It arises as a need to seek financial solutions to traditional economic models. The impact of the world economy must be combated using the possibilities offered by cryptocurrencies, among other possibilities.

A clear example of this affectation was the real estate bubble that occurred in 2008, generating effects in many other economic sectors and ultimately a global economic recession.
